Welcome to the Iden2 Help Center! We're here to assist you in making the most of your identity verification experience. Below are common topics and resources to help you resolve any issues or learn more about the app's features.

1. Getting Started

How do I create an account?
  • Download the Iden2 app from your app store.
  • Open the app and follow the on-screen prompts to set up your account.
  • You will be asked to verify your identity by providing personal details such as your name, government-issued ID, and biometric data.
  • Once verified, your digital identity is created and ready to use for secure access across our supported services.
How do I verify my identity?
  • Tap the "Verify Identity" button on the home screen.
  • Follow the prompts to scan your ID, provide a selfie, and complete the biometric authentication.
  • Once the verification process is complete, you will be notified of your verified status.
My identity verification is taking too long.
  • Most verifications are completed within a few minutes. However, in rare cases, it may take longer due to high demand or technical issues.
  • If your verification is pending for more than 24 hours, please contact support using the options below.
I received an error message during identity verification.
  • Ensure that the ID you uploaded is valid and clearly visible.
  • Ensure that your selfie matches your ID photo and is well-lit.
  • If the problem persists, contact support for further assistance.

3. Managing Your Account

How do I update my personal information?
  • To update your personal details such as your email, phone number, or address, go to "Account Settings" and follow the prompts to make changes.
  • Some information, like your verified identity details, may require additional verification to update.
How do I delete my account?
  • If you wish to delete your Iden2 account, navigate to "Account Settings" and select "Delete Account".
  • Please note that once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

4. Using Iden2 with Other Services

How do I use Iden2 for identity verification on partner apps or websites?
  • Once your identity is verified, you can use Iden2 to authenticate and verify your identity on supported platforms.
  • Simply select "Sign in with Iden2" or scan the QR code on the partner site.
What services can I use with Iden2?
  • Iden2 is integrated with various service providers, including financial institutions, car rentals, and online services.
  • Check the app's "Partner Services" section for the latest supported services.

5. Need Further Assistance?

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please reach out to our support team. Contact Support at support@iden2.com.
